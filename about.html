About Simply Nerdy

Your hub for video games, books, movies, and TV shows

Welcome!

Simply Nerdy is a YouTube channel where we dive deep into the things we love: epic video games, incredible books (especially Brandon Sanderson's Cosmere), blockbuster movies, and binge-worthy TV shows. Whether you're looking for game reviews, book discussions, or passionate debates about the latest shows, you'll find content here that's both entertaining and thought-provoking.

What We Cover

Video Games

Reviews, gameplay analysis, and discussions about the latest and greatest games across all platforms.

Books & Literature

Deep dives into fantasy and sci-fi novels, with a special focus on Brandon Sanderson's Cosmere universe.

Movies

Reviews, theories, and breakdowns of blockbusters, indie films, and everything in between.

TV Shows

Episode recaps, season reviews, and passionate discussions about your favorite series.

Our Mission

Our mission is to celebrate geek culture in all its forms. We believe that entertainment should spark conversation, inspire imagination, and bring people together. Through honest reviews, thoughtful analysis, and a healthy dose of nerdy enthusiasm, we aim to share our passion for the stories and worlds we love.
